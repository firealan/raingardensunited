Butterflyway Parade

16 Sep 2017

Join us on Saturday, September 16, 2017 as we celebrate local bees, butterflies, birds and bats, and the first year of the David Suzuki Foundation's national The Butterflyway Project. 

Our musical parade will take us along the boardwalk from the Woodbine Bathing Station to Kew Gardens, where there will be food and drink vendors, community groups and art and craft activities.
